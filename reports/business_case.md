# Personal Finance Intelligence System

## Problem
Users struggle to monitor spending patterns and predict financial behavior.

## Solution
Built a financial intelligence platform using analytics,
forecasting, anomaly detection, and KPI monitoring.

## Features
- Financial KPI dashboard
- Spending analytics
- Anomaly detection
- Expense forecasting
- Financial health scoring

## Business Impact
- Improved financial visibility
- Identified abnormal spending behavior
- Forecasted future liabilities
- Enabled proactive budgeting
